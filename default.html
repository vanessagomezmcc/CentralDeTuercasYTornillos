About Us

Trusted Industrial Partner for Over Three Decades

Founded in 1988, CTT has proudly served the industrial sector for over 35 years, establishing itself as one of the leading industrial machinery and replacement parts suppliers in Honduras. Based in San Pedro Sula, the industrial hub of the country, CTT has built a reputation for reliability, competitive pricing, and unmatched sourcing capabilities across Central America and the United States.

Through strong international supplier relationships and an extensive distribution network, the company consistently delivers high-quality machinery and components tailored to the needs of manufacturing, energy, logistics, construction, and agro-industrial operations.
